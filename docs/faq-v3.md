# Birthday Bot v3 — FAQ

## Is v3 confirmed?
No. It is currently a design and validation phase.

## Will v3 replace v2 immediately?
No. There will be no abrupt switch.

## Will my data be lost?
The goal is to migrate existing data, but no blind promises are made.

## Will premium still exist?
Yes, but in a clearer and fairer form.

## Will the bot remain usable without premium?
Yes. Always.

## Why is this taking time?
Because stability and respect for users matter more than speed.

## Can I contribute?
Not yet. Feedback is welcome, implementation is intentionally limited.

## Why create a new repo?
To avoid breaking v2 and to work on a clean foundation.
